    b3scale - Multi tenant load balancer for BigBlueButton™

    b3scale is a load balancer designed to be used in place of scalelite. It was started and is maintained by infra.run to provide multiple features not available before. b3scale is written in Go, making it easy to deploy, easy to observe and easy to scale.
